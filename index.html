Cricket is a team sport that involves batting, fielding, and bowling, and is played with a bat and ball on an oval-shaped field

History

Cricket originated in England and is popular in countries that were once ruled by England, including India, Pakistan, Australia, and South Africa.

Popularity

Cricket is popular in countries that England once ruled, such as India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Australia, the West Indies, and South Africa.
