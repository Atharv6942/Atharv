Rules of Cricket

1. The number of players in each team is 11 players.
2. If the ball is too far for the batsman to hit it is called a wide and if the ball goes anywhere to the left of the batsman it is a wide.
3. If the bowler crosses the crease and bowls, it is called a no ball.
4. In t20 and ODI cricket if it is a no ball the batsman gets a free hit wich means he cant get out unless stumped or run-out

Ways to Get Out in Cricket

There are total of ten ways to get out in cricket and here they are

  1. Bowled
  2. Caught out
  3. Run-Out
  4. Hit wicket
  5. Ball handling
  6. Stumped
  7. Hit the ball twice
  8. LBW (leg before wicket)
  9. Timed out
  10. Obstructing the field-cricket dismissal that occurs when a batter intentionally prevents the bowling team from taking a catch, fielding, throwing, or hitting the stumps

How to score runs

  1. The batsman can score runs by running between the wickets
  2. When the batsman hits the ball and it touches the boundary with bouncing it is Four runs
  3. When the batsman hits the ball hard and it goes over the boundary without bouncing it is Six runs
